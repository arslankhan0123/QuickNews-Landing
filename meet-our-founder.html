Meet Our Team

Ali (Founder)

Quick News Global (AI) isn't just a news app, there lies a profound belief that "access to clear, unbiased information" is a cornerstone of connection and progress. My own journey, beginning in a world where the struggle for basic necessities was constant, deeply ingrained in me the value of knowledge and the power of a collective, well-informed society. From early childhood, working alongside my father to make ends meet, and later navigating the challenges of self-funded education in my home country and then in Europe, I witnessed firsthand how crucial information, or the lack thereof, shaped destinies. The personal hardships, while immense, forged an unshakeable determination to contribute something truly meaningful to the world.

This vision crystallized into a powerful question, "In our hyper-connected yet often fragmented world, how can we truly bring people together through the news?" The answer lies not just in quantity, but in "quality, accessibility, and speed."

Powered by AI, Guided by Purpose, We recognized that the traditional news landscape, hard to navigate UI/UX, burdened by information overload, sensationalism, and time constraints, often hinders true understanding. This is where our innovative approach comes in. Our news app with easy to navigate UI/UX, leverages advanced AI to distill complex articles into concise, impartial summaries. This isn't about replacing critical thought but empowering it. Our AI acts as your intelligent guide, cutting through the noise to deliver the core facts and diverse perspectives you need, faster than ever before. This technological leap allows you to stay informed efficiently, connect with the essence of global events, and form your own well-rounded opinions without bias.

Yes, its not just headlines, Uniting Through Understanding

Our mission extends far beyond just delivering news. We believe that by providing clear, digestible information, we can foster empathy, facilitate informed discussions, and ultimately bring communities closer. In a world often divided by narratives, our AI-powered summaries offer a neutral ground, presenting information objectively so that understanding can flourish. We envision a future where everyone, regardless of their background or busy schedule, can easily access the insights that shape our world, leading to more connected and collaborative societies.

Our Global Aspiration, News for Every Hand, Support from the Heart

This is just the beginning. Our ambition is to expand our reach to every country, every culture, every cell phone across the globe. We are committed to continuous innovation, refining our AI capabilities, and diversifying our content to serve an ever-growing international audience. As we scale, our promise remains unwavering, "to provide not only cutting-edge technology but also unparalleled human connection." I am personally dedicated to ensuring the highest level of customer support, believing that a supportive community is just as vital as innovative technology. My journey taught me the importance of resilience and connection, and these principles are woven into the very fabric of our app and our commitment to you.

Join us as we build a more informed, connected, and understanding world, one summary at a time.
