About us

We are a bootstrapped AI-powered news app dedicated to providing quality, concise news that matters to everyone. Our goal is to simplify information consumption, reduce screen time, and empower individuals to stay informed & united while focusing on what truly matters—family, community, and personal growth.

Our Mission

To empower every individual with quick, meaningful access to the news that matters—using AI to simplify information, reduce screen time, and help people stay informed while spending more time with their families and communities.

Our Vision

To build a globally trusted AI-powered news platform that connects people through shared knowledge, fosters unity, and transforms how the world consumes information—because when people are informed, they are stronger together.

Our Support Promise

At Quick News Global (AI), we pride ourselves on providing personalized support directly overseen by our founder. We are committed to ensuring that every individual receives the best possible experience, knowing that their concerns are heard and addressed directly by our leadership team.
